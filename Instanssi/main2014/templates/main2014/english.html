What is Instanssi?

Instanssi is a festival of digital creativity in Jyväskylä, Finland. It is organized on March 1.-3. 2013 at Voionmaan lukio. Instanssi is meant to be a beginner friendly event, which everyone who is interested in digital culture can visit and enjoy. Instanssi offers workshops, presentations and friendly creative environment. This year we concentrate especially on games development and design. Unfortunately most of the page is currently only available in Finnish.

You can get more info on our IRC channel at #instanssi (on IRCnet).

The address of the venue is Voionmaankatu 17-19, 40700 Jyväskylä, Finland.
